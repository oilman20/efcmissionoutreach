EFC EVANGELISTIC MISSION.

(Non Denominational)

An Outreach Based Ministry. A Non Profit making Religious Organisation. A Non Denominational Outreach. Founded by A.S Chukwudi in Aug 8, 2018, Efc Mission Outreach (EFCMO) exists to proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ and disciple men through Biblical truths towards a Total World Evangelization and Every available means as LED by the Holy Spirit. To Help and equip the Church in effective Evangelism and Soul Winning. The Evangelistic Missions Operation would be directed from it's Headquarters or Wherever the team Would meet for it activities. Through the Online and On ground Platform meetings on how to strategies towards Soul Winning Would be Upheld. Towns, Villages, Communities, Cities and Nations are places we look Forward to Outreach, Roots where Christ has not been Known and Places where Souls are Ripe for the Gospel truth. Revival Programs, Prayer meetings, Crusades and Evangelistic Missions and any other platform for soul Winning are Open to be Undertaken For Evangelistic Mission Outreaches. To Reach men at all Cost Even if it cost Us our Lives.

AIMS AND OBJECTIVES.

  • To Proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ and to disciple men through Biblical truths towards a Total World Evangelization.
  • To Win Million of Souls through Evangelistic Missions.
  • To Help and Equip the Church in Effective Evangelism and Soul Winning Business.
  • Towns, Villages, Communities, Cities and Nations are places We Look forward to Outreaching.
  • Efc Strategic meeting is geared towards Equipping and training believers so as to be Effective in Soul winning.
  • Holiness of Life is Paramount to Us as a mission Outreach.
